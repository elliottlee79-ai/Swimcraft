What is Swimcraft?

Swimcraft is a coarse-fishing simulator with a difference: you turn real photographs of your favourite swims into fully playable venues — then fish them.

It's in active development. Here's what's playable now, and what's on the way.

Photo-to-venue

Trace a swim from your own photos — water, islands, depths and features — and it becomes a fishable peg.

Real cast & fight

An honest cast, line management, and a species-accurate fight where fish run, tire, and test your tackle.

Tackle & bait

Rods, reels, lines, hooks, rigs and baits that matter — starting simple and unlocking as you progress.

Angler progression

Level up, log your records, and build out your tackle box over time.

Tournaments Planned

Online tournaments and a shared match calendar are planned for after launch — not in the game yet.

Leaderboards Planned

Venue and global leaderboards are on the roadmap, not live today.

Prefer email?

Wishlisting on Steam is the best way to hear at launch — but leave your email for the occasional update.
