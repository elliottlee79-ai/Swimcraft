What is Swimcraft?

Swimcraft is a next‑generation fishing simulator where you turn real photographs of your favourite fishing spots into fully playable venues — complete with realistic fish AI, tackle systems, angler progression, online tournaments, fish record systems and more.

Inspired by the legendary FishSim games of the early 2000s, Swimcraft brings authentic angling simulation to modern platforms with a focus on realism, creativity, and community.

Features

Create Venues

Upload a photo, paint depths, stock species, and publish your own fishing venues.

Realistic Fishing

Float, ledger, spinning — with accurate rod behaviour, bite windows, and fish AI.

Community Records

Compete for venue records, named specimens, and seasonal leaderboards.

Cross‑Platform

Available on Windows, Android, and Apple platforms.

Player Progression

Earn your status from novice to professional as you master each discipline.

Endless Variety

Stock the water with any fish you want — even upload your own fish images.

All Fishing Methods

Float fishing, feeder, carp fishing, spinning, and even sea fishing.

Live Tournaments

Compete in online events with real‑life prizes and global leaderboards.
